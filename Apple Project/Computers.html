    Timeline of Computers

    Apple 1

    The Apple 1 was released on July 1, 1976. The Apple 1 sold for $666.66 which is equivalent to $2,777 in 2015. The Apple 1 was discontinued on September 1, 1977.

    Apple 2

    The Apple 2 was released in June, 1977 and cost $1,298. This amount is equivalent to $5,077 in 2015. The Apple 2 was discontinued on June 1, 1979.

    Apple 3

    The Apple 3 was released on September 1, 1980. The Apple 3 cost between $4340 and $7800. This is equivalent between $12485.39 and $22439.18 in 2015. The Apple 3 was discontinued on December 1, 1981.

    Apple Lisa

    The Apple Lisa was released on January 1, 1983 and cost $9,995. This amount is equivalent to $23,800 in 2015. The Apple Lisa was discontinued on January 1, 1984.

    Macintosh

    The Macintosh was released on January 24, 1984. The Macintosh was sold for $2,495 which is equivalent to $5,692 in 2015. The Macintosh was discontinued on September 10, 1984.

    Macintosh 2

    The Macintosh 2 was released on March 2, 1987. The Macintosh 2 was sold for $5,500 which is equivalent to $11,475 in 2015. The Macintosh 2 was discontinued on January 15, 1990.

    Macintosh Classic 2

    The Macintosh Classic 2 was released on October 21, 1991. The Macintosh Classic 2 sold for $1900 which is equivalent to $3306.55 in 2015. The Macintosh Classic 2 was discontinued on September 13, 1993.

    iMac G3

    The iMac G3 was released on August 15, 1998. The iMac G3 was discontinued on May 10, 1999.

    iMac G4

    The iMac G4 was released on January 7, 2002. The iMac G4 was discontinued on August 31, 2004.

    Macbook Air

    The Macbook Air was released on January 8, 2008. Apple still continues to make the Macbook Air but over the years they have updated it to the version we see today.
