    The History of Apple Computers

    In 1976, Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak started Apple in Steve's parents garage. Steve Wozniak was a genius when it came to computers. He was the only human to ever design and build a computer from scratch. Steve Jobs made good use of Steve Wozniak's talent by turning it into the most profitable company at the time of Steve Job's death
